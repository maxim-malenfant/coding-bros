Our Coding Journey üöÄ

How It Started

After crushing a chest day, we were sitting in the gym parking lot talking about the future. "Bro, what if we could build apps as sick as our physiques?" Chad said. Brad replied, "Dude, let's do it!" And just like that, Gym Bros Code was born.

The Early Days

We started with HTML and CSS while sipping our post-workout shakes. "This is just like meal prep," Brad said, "you need the right ingredients in the right order." We built our first website - a simple gym tracker. It looked terrible, but it was OURS.

Leveling Up

JavaScript hit us harder than our first set of squats. But just like in the gym, we didn't quit. We learned that coding, like working out, is all about consistency, patience, and the occasional rage against a bug that makes no sense.

The Breakthrough

After 3 months, we built our first real project - a workout tracking app with a clean UI and smooth animations. The moment it worked flawlessly, we felt the same rush as hitting a new PR. We were officially gym bros who code!

Now

6 months in, and we're building full-stack applications, learning React, diving into backend development with Node.js, and even dabbling in mobile apps. Our post-gym sessions have become sacred coding time. The endorphins from working out fuel our creativity and problem-solving!
